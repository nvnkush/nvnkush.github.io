Naveen Kushwaha

PhD (Chemical Engineering, IIT Roorkee)

Dynamic professional with a doctoral degree in Chemical Engineering and over 5 years of research experience in fluid dynamics, heat transfer, and particles flow dynamics. Currently serving as a Senior Process Engineer at Dr. Reddy's Laboratory, specializing in Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) models for optimizing pharmaceutical manufacturing processes.

Experience

Senior Process Engineer (Feb. 2023 - Present)
Dr. Reddy’s Laboratory, Hyderabad, INDIA

  • Developing Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) models for optimizing various manufacturing processes.
  • Collaborating with cross-functional teams to drive process improvement initiatives.
  • Utilizing the Discrete Element Method (DEM) approach for developing particle dynamics models.
  • Conducting case studies both in the laboratory and at plant scale.
