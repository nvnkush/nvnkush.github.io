Naveen Kushwaha

PhD (Chemical Engineering, IIT Roorkee)

Dynamic professional with a doctoral degree in Chemical Engineering and over 5 years of research experience in fluid dynamics, heat transfer, and particles flow dynamics. Currently serving as a Senior Process Engineer at Dr. Reddy's Laboratory, specializing in Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) models for optimizing pharmaceutical manufacturing processes.
